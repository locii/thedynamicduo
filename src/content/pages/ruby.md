## Classical guitarist

Ruby Cheyne is a guitarist studying at the **Melbourne Conservatorium of Music**. A dedicated classical player, she has been featured on air and recognised in competition at both state and national level — a track record that speaks to a mature, reliable musicianship well beyond her years.

## Highlights

- Studying guitar at the **Melbourne Conservatorium of Music**
- Performed on **3MBS** (Melbourne's fine music radio)
- Prize-winner in **Victorian and national competitions**

## Her approach

Ruby brings a poised, attentive classical touch to everything she plays — the kind of clean, considered technique that suits a wedding ceremony or a refined corporate setting perfectly. In lessons, she's patient and methodical, with a strong grounding in classical fundamentals.
