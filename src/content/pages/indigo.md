## Singer-songwriter & guitarist

Indigo Sangster is a singer-songwriter and classical guitarist currently studying **contemporary guitar at the Melbourne Conservatorium of Music**. Her playing moves fluently between the discipline of classical guitar and the feel of contemporary songwriting — a range that makes her equally suited to a quiet ceremony and a lively reception.

## Highlights

- Studying **contemporary guitar** at the Melbourne Conservatorium of Music
- **Classical guitarist** with a songwriter's ear for melody and arrangement
- Exhibited at **Top Arts 2025**
- Previous winner, **Australian Children's Music Foundation (ACMF)** — for an original children's song

## Her approach

As a songwriter, Indigo listens for the story in a piece before she plays it. That instinct carries into her performing and her teaching — an emphasis on musicality and expression, not just the notes on the page.
