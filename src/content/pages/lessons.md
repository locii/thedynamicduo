## Learn from Conservatorium-trained guitarists

Both Indigo and Ruby teach guitar, sharing the technique and musicianship they've developed at the **Melbourne Conservatorium of Music**. Lessons are warm, structured and tailored to you — whether you're picking up the guitar for the first time or refining an advanced classical repertoire.

## Who it's for

- **Absolute beginners** — a friendly, encouraging start with the fundamentals
- **Improvers** — build technique, theory and repertoire with clear direction
- **Advanced & exam students** — classical technique, interpretation and performance craft
- **All ages** — children and adults both welcome

## What you can learn

- **Classical guitar** — technique, repertoire and reading
- **Contemporary guitar** — chords, accompaniment and playing your favourite songs
- **Musicianship** — theory, timing and ear training woven through every lesson

## Choose your teacher

- **Lessons with Indigo** — a songwriter's approach, strong on contemporary playing and musical expression
- **Lessons with Ruby** — a classical focus, methodical and grounded in solid fundamentals
