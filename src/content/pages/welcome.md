## Elegant guitar, for the moments that matter

The Dynamic Duo is **Indigo Sangster** and **Ruby Cheyne** — two young guitarists trained at the Melbourne Conservatorium of Music. Together they bring a refined blend of classical repertoire and contemporary covers to weddings, ceremonies, corporate functions and private events.

Whether you're planning the soundtrack to a wedding aisle, a relaxed drinks reception, or a polished corporate evening, we tailor the set to the room.

## What we offer

- **Live performance** — weddings, ceremonies, corporate events and private functions
- **Guitar lessons** — classical and contemporary tuition for all ages and levels
- **A flexible repertoire** — from classical guitar to tasteful contemporary arrangements

> Trained musicians. Considered arrangements. A calm, professional presence on the day.
