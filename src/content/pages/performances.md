## Music tailored to your event

The Dynamic Duo performs live for weddings, corporate functions and private events across Melbourne and Victoria. Two guitars give a fuller, warmer sound than a solo player — refined enough for a ceremony, engaging enough for a reception — and the programme is always shaped around your occasion.

## Weddings

- **Ceremony** — a processional, signing and recessional chosen with you
- **Pre-drinks & canapés** — relaxed background music as guests arrive
- **Reception** — a lift in energy with contemporary favourites

## Corporate & private events

- Product launches, galas, awards nights and cocktail functions
- Restaurant and venue background music
- Private parties and milestone celebrations

## Repertoire

Our repertoire spans two worlds, mixed to suit the room:

- **Classical** — elegant guitar works and arrangements, ideal for ceremonies and refined settings
- **Contemporary covers** — tasteful arrangements of well-loved songs, old and new

Have a special request? We're happy to learn a **first-dance song** or a piece that means something to you, where time allows.
