## Two players, one voice

Indigo Sangster and Ruby Cheyne met through their shared study of the guitar at the **Melbourne Conservatorium of Music**. What began as a friendship over shared repertoire has grown into a performing duo equally at home with a Bach transcription and a contemporary ballad.

Their playing draws on serious classical foundations — technique, tone and interpretation honed at one of Australia's leading music schools — paired with an ear for the songs people actually want to hear on the day.

## Why book the duo

- **Two guitars, more texture** — richer arrangements than a solo player, without the volume of a full band
- **Versatile programming** — classical for a ceremony, contemporary for the reception
- **Professional and prepared** — reliable timing, a considered set list, and a calm presence

The result is music that feels intentional — never background filler, never overpowering.
